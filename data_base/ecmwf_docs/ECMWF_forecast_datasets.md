# Datasets

Search

ECMWF produce and disseminate weather forecast data for the National Meteorological and Hydrological Services (NHMSs) of [ECMWF Member and Co-operating States](https://www.ecmwf.int/en/about/who-we-are/member-states) and their authorised users.

ECMWF also archives data and makes this available to authorised users. Some data is also made available under licence and some is publicly available.

See also

[Parameter database](https://codes.ecmwf.int/grib/param-db/)
